-- Blackbush Powder ----- 
   Grind Blackbush Stem into Blackbush Powder using Mortar and Pestle.
-- Gobo Salve ----- 
   Combine Gobo Oil, Blackbush Powder, into 0 Unfinished Gobo Salve.
   Heat Unfinished Gobo Salve into Gobo Salve using Pot.
-- Lavender Salve ----- 
   Combine Lavender Oil, Sicklepod Powder, into 0 Unfinished Lavender Salve.
   Heat Unfinished Lavender Salve into Lavender Salve using Pot.
-- Sicklepod Powder ----- 
   Grind Sicklepod Flower into Sicklepod Powder using Mortar and Pestle.
